Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Retro Clock Screensaver ("we", "our", or "app") handles your information.

Information Collection

Our app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app functions entirely offline as a screensaver/clock display and does not require any personal data to operate.

App Permissions

The app only requires basic system permissions to function as a screensaver. These include:

  • Display over other apps (for screensaver functionality)
  • Run at startup (optional, for screensaver activation)

Data Storage

The app only stores your display preferences (such as color settings) locally on your device. This information never leaves your device and is only used to remember your preferred display settings.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services or analytics tools in our app.
